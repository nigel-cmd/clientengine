## CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

### Variables to Replace

- [FIRST_NAME] — Prospect's first name
- [COMPANY_NAME] — Their company name
- [YOUR_NAME] — Your name
- [YOUR_TITLE] — Your title/position
- [CALENDAR_LINK] — Your booking link (Calendly, etc.)
- [PAIN_POINT] — Specific problem you solve
- [DESIRED_OUTCOME] — Result you deliver
- [SIMILAR_COMPANY] — Case study/example client
- [SPECIFIC_RESULT] — Concrete numbers/outcomes

### Tone Adjustments

**For corporate/conservative industries:**
- Remove emojis
- Use full sentences
- More formal language
- Focus on ROI/business outcomes

**For creative/startup industries:**
- Can use emojis sparingly
- Shorter sentences
- Conversational tone
- Focus on innovation/growth

**For professional services:**
- Balance of formal and friendly
- Emphasize credibility/expertise
- Include credentials where relevant

---

## USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

1. **Customize all bracketed variables** before sending
2. **Personalize the first sentence** — show you did research
3. **Keep subject lines under 50 characters** when possible
4. **Send from your professional email** (not generic company address)
5. **Track opens/clicks** to optimize timing and messaging
6. **A/B test subject lines** — small changes = big differences
